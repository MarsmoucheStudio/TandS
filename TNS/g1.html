TURKEY AND SQUIRREL
Animal Dystopia


First, you need to know what mode you want to play in. You have the choice between normal and competitive mode, to become the first of the leaderboard. If you want to play in competitive mode click on load then enter the code A1.


Then you need to know how to interact in the game. the movements are WASD and space, E to interact with objects and O to save.



Classes

Turkey- turkeys can jump 0.03% higher than squirrels and do 0.002% more damage when attacking with a weapon. the turkey is great for fight.


Squirrel- squirrels walk 0.05% faster than turkeys and have a 0.002% more chance of finding minerals than turkey.


Hollyday guy- 5% more chance to find minerals, 5% more monster, 0.9% resistance to monster attack and 10% more snow zone.

DLC - downloadable content

In Turkey and Squirrel you have acces to other Class than the Turkey and the Squirrel. These other Class are the Narwhal and the Witch Doctor. To use a DLC Class you need to use the Trade option on this website (the Trade Form is on the main Turkey and Squirrel page). If you want to know how to trade a DLC go to the next section.
Both Narwhal and Witch Doctor are some strange creature with a Pumkin head, to use them in their true form you need wear some different Armor.

1- The Narwhal To be a Narwhal you need to use the Cat Helm (Gold Helm). The Narwhal give a +5% chance to find more minerals and +0.5% DMG.
2- The Witch Doctor To be a Witch Doctor you need to use the Armadillo helm (Diamond Helm).
The Witch Doctor have a +0.5% Receive DMG reduce.
